Guide for Parents

Tips for Each Stage

Stage 1 (Ages 5-7): Wonder & Curiosity

  • Let your child experiment freely with AI tools
  • Ask questions like "What do you think will happen?"
  • Celebrate their discoveries and creativity
  • Focus on the fun and magical aspects of AI

Stage 2 (Ages 7-10): Building Skills

  • Encourage problem-solving and logical thinking
  • Help them break down complex tasks into steps
  • Praise persistence and effort over perfection
  • Connect coding concepts to real-world activities

Stage 3 (Ages 8-10): Creating & Innovating

  • Support their creative projects and ideas
  • Help them understand AI's limitations and capabilities
  • Discuss responsible AI use and digital citizenship
  • Encourage them to share their creations with others

Safety & Best Practices

Supervision

Always supervise young children when using AI tools

Privacy

Don't share personal information with AI tools

Communication

Talk about what AI can and cannot do

Screen Time

Balance AI activities with other learning experiences
